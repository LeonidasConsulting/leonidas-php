For over two decades, we have built our reputation on a single principle: your technology should work for you, not against you. We do not chase buzzwords. We deploy what works, we stand behind it, and we stay accountable when it does not.

## What We Do

Leonidas delivers six core service lines, each backed by deep technical expertise and a commitment to doing things right the first time.

### Managed IT Services

Proactive monitoring, patch management, endpoint management, helpdesk support, and strategic IT planning. We operate as your outsourced IT department or as an extension of your existing team. Our managed IT services include:

- IT asset lifecycle management and hardware procurement
- Disaster recovery planning, backup architecture, and DR testing
- Cloud migration strategy and hybrid IT architecture design
- Microsoft 365 administration and optimization
- Technology roadmap development aligned to business goals and budget cycles
- Proactive vs. reactive IT — we fix problems before they impact your business

### Cybersecurity

We build layered security programs that go beyond checkbox compliance. Our cybersecurity services include:

- Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) and Managed Detection and Response (MDR)
- Dark web monitoring and credential exposure alerting
- DNS-layer security (Cisco Umbrella, Cloudflare Gateway, DNSFilter)
- Email security with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configuration
- Privileged Access Management (PAM) and least-privilege enforcement
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) deployment and enforcement
- Security awareness training with phishing simulations
- Vulnerability assessments and penetration testing coordination
- Compliance framework alignment: NIST CSF, CIS Controls, CMMC
- Virtual CISO (vCISO) services for executive-level security leadership
- Cyber insurance readiness assessments

### Network Engineering

From initial design to implementation and ongoing optimization, we architect networks built for performance, security, and growth:

- SD-WAN and SASE deployment and management
- Network segmentation, VLAN design, and microsegmentation
- Wireless network design with professional site surveys
- QoS configuration for VoIP and real-time applications
- Network Access Control (NAC)
- Firewall deployment, configuration, and ongoing management
- Network monitoring, alerting, and NOC services
- Wi-Fi 7 and next-generation wireless planning

### Unified Communications & VoIP

We design, deploy, and support cloud phone systems and unified communications platforms. Our team handles every aspect of a UC deployment:

- Platform evaluation and selection
- Network readiness assessment and QoS configuration
- Number porting and call flow design
- Auto-attendant, IVR, and call queue configuration
- Contact center deployment (RingCX, Five9)
- SIP trunking implementation
- IP phone and softphone provisioning
- End-user training and adoption support

**Platform expertise:** RingCentral, RingCX, Microsoft Teams Phone, Five9, Zoom Phone, 8x8, Vonage, Dialpad, Nextiva, GoTo Connect, Mitel, and others.

### Telecom & WAN Solutions

We help businesses evaluate, procure, and manage their connectivity:

- Fiber and broadband circuit procurement and negotiation
- MPLS to SD-WAN migration planning and execution
- POTS line replacement for elevator phones, alarms, fax, and gate access
- Telecom expense management and bill auditing
- 5G and Fixed Wireless Access (FWA) evaluation
- Multi-carrier redundancy and failover design
- Access to 300+ carriers and connectivity vendors

### Desktop Support

Fast, reliable support for workstations, laptops, printers, and end-user applications. We resolve hardware and software issues, manage device deployments, handle OS upgrades and driver conflicts, and keep your workforce productive. Whether remote or on-site, our support team responds quickly and resolves issues the first time.
